## üö® Escalation Matrix

- VA not responding to messages
- Minor technical issues
- Schedule changes needed
- Routine welfare check

### Level 2: Team Lead (Within 24h)
- VA marked orange for 2+ days
- Client communication slow
- Performance concerns raised
- Workload complaints

### Level 3: Operations Manager (Within 48h)
- VA marked red
- Client expressing dissatisfaction
- Resignation mentioned
- Technical issues unresolved 1+ week
